SECURITY TIPS

Essential technical guardrails for staying safe online

Digital Security Basics

  • Use strong, unique passwords
  • Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
  • Keep software & OS updated
  • Download apps from official stores only
  • Be cautious of suspicious links and emails
  • Back up your data regularly

☎Phone safety

  • use a screen lock
  • be cautious of public Wi-Fi
  • keep your phone updated
  • back up your phone data regularly

🌐Online behavior

  • think before you click
  • be mindful of what you share online
  • respect others' privacy
  • report harmful content

💳Financial safety

  • monitor your accounts regularly
  • be cautious of unsolicited calls or emails
  • use secure payment methods
  • shred sensitive documents before disposal

🧍‍♂️Personal Safety

  • be aware of your surroundings
  • trust your instincts
  • avoid walking alone in unfamiliar areas
  • keep emergency contacts readily available
