SECURITY TIPS

Essential technical guardrails for staying safe online

Digital Security Basics

  • Use strong, unique passwords
  • Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
  • Keep software & OS updated
  • Download apps from official stores only
  • Be cautious of suspicious links and emails
  • Back up your data regularly

Phone Safety

  • Use a screen lock (PIN/biometric)
  • Avoid public Wi-Fi for sensitive transactions
  • Keep your phone OS updated
  • Back up phone data weekly

Online Behavior

  • Think before you click
  • Be mindful of what you share
  • Respect others' privacy
  • Report harmful content immediately

Financial Safety

  • Monitor bank accounts regularly
  • Be cautious of unsolicited calls/emails
  • Use secure payment methods (Mobile Money with PIN)
  • Shred sensitive documents before disposal

Personal Safety

  • Be aware of your surroundings
  • Trust your instincts
  • Avoid isolated areas at night
  • Keep emergency contacts ready
